Welcome to the Trivia Train!

Hop on board the Trivia Train! Trivia Train is a fun trivia game to play solo or with friends and family. You simply select a category from the list provided and hit the "Let's Play" button, which generates a trivia question within that category. Hit the "Reveal Answer" button when you have come up with an answer to see if you got it right or wrong! Paired with the answer will be a fun GIF related to the answer.
